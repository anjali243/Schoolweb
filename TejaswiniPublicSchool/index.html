Quality Education
The Key to Unlocking a Better Future

Tejaswini Public School is a private English medium institution located in Damoh, Madhya Pradesh. We are committed to providing quality education from Nursery to Class VIII in a safe, disciplined, and nurturing environment. Our aim is to develop confident, responsible, and well-rounded students through academic excellence and strong moral values.

About Our School

Tejaswini Public School focuses on holistic education by combining academic learning with character building, discipline, and life skills to prepare students for future challenges.

Classes Offered

We offer education from Nursery, LKG, UKG, and Classes I to VIII, following a structured curriculum suitable for each stage of learning.

Qualified Faculty

Our experienced and dedicated teachers ensure individual attention, concept clarity, and continuous academic progress for every student.

English Medium Education

The medium of instruction is English, helping students develop strong communication skills while maintaining a solid academic foundation.

Safe Learning Environment

We provide a secure, supportive, and student-friendly atmosphere that encourages learning, creativity, and personal growth.

Overall Student Development

Along with academics, we emphasize discipline, moral values, and co-curricular activities for the all-round development of student.
